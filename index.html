Executive Function Assessment (Adults 18+)

Executive functioning is the set of mental skills your brain uses to get things done: controlling impulses, holding information, managing emotion, starting and focusing, planning, staying organized, adapting, and more. Everyone is stronger in some than others.

This is our full assessment. It reads fourteen skills, grouped into four areas, and shows you a complete map of your strengths and growth areas. About 5 minutes. Answer honestly, accuracy depends on it. You get a personalised read at the end.

This is an educational self-reflection tool, not a diagnosis or clinical assessment. It does not diagnose ADHD or any condition. Kaizen Education Services provides coaching.
